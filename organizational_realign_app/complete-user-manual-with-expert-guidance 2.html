2. Patent-Pending Algorithm Details

Our 6 proprietary algorithms are mathematical models specifically designed for organizational analysis, developed in-house over thousands of data points and years of R&D.

üî¨ OCI‚Ñ¢ (Organizational Complexity Index)

Purpose: Quantifies structural friction and measures role clarity vs. strategic alignment

How it works: Analyzes reporting relationships, role definitions, and decision-making pathways to calculate a complexity score from 0-100

Expert interpretation: Scores above 75 indicate high complexity requiring structural intervention with change management support

üè• HOCI‚Ñ¢ (Hierarchical Optimization Coefficient Index)

Purpose: Measures decision-making efficiency and departmental clarity (healthcare-adapted version)

How it works: Evaluates hierarchical layers, spans of control, and decision approval pathways

Expert interpretation: Identifies optimal flattening opportunities while maintaining clinical oversight requirements

üìã JCI‚Ñ¢ (Job Clarity Index)

Purpose: Evaluates role definition, accountability, and process transparency

How it works: Analyzes job descriptions, reporting relationships, and process documentation completeness

Expert interpretation: Low scores indicate role confusion requiring organizational redesign with stakeholder engagement

‚öñÔ∏è DSCH (Decisional Span of Control Heuristic)

Purpose: Optimizes reporting structures and management capacity

How it works: Calculates ideal manager-to-direct-report ratios based on role complexity and industry benchmarks

Expert interpretation: Provides specific rebalancing recommendations with leadership development support

üì° CRF (Communication Resource Framework)

Purpose: Identifies communication bottlenecks and inefficiencies

How it works: Maps information flow pathways and identifies redundant or missing communication channels

Expert interpretation: Recommends communication restructuring with cultural change management strategies

üëî LEI (Leadership Effectiveness Index)

Purpose: Assesses management capacity and organizational effectiveness

How it works: Evaluates leadership spans, decision authority, and team performance metrics

Expert interpretation: Identifies leadership development needs and succession planning opportunities

11. Troubleshooting & Support

Common Issues and Solutions

Assessment Questions

Problem: Unclear how to answer specific questions

Solution: Each question includes help text and examples. Contact support for clarification or schedule expert consultation.

Problem: Questions don't apply to our organization

Solution: Select "Not Applicable" or use comment fields to provide context. Our expert review will adjust analysis accordingly.

Data Upload

Problem: File upload failures or format issues

Solution: Ensure files are under size limits (50MB per file). Supported formats: PDF, Excel, Word, CSV. Contact support for assistance.

Problem: Sensitive data concerns

Solution: Remove personal identifiers before upload. Our algorithms work with structural data, not individual information.

Team Collaboration

Problem: Team member can't access invitation

Solution: Check spam/junk folders. Resend invitation or manually add member through team dashboard.

Problem: Permission conflicts or access issues

Solution: Admin can adjust roles and permissions in team settings. Contact support for complex permission scenarios.

Report Generation

Problem: Report taking longer than expected

Solution: Complex analyses may require additional processing time. You'll receive email notification when complete.
