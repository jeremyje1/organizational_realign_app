How long does the assessment take to complete?
The assessment length varies by tier: 50 questions for Higher Ed AI Pulse Check, 105 questions for AI Readiness Comprehensive, and 150-200 questions for AI Transformation Blueprint and Enterprise Partnership tiers. Completion time ranges from 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on tier and depth of responses. We recommend allowing extra time for thoughtful responses and team consultation.
Can multiple people contribute to one assessment?
Yes! Higher-tier assessments include team collaboration features. You can invite colleagues to contribute their expertise to specific sections, ensuring comprehensive and accurate responses across all domains.
How often should we repeat the assessment?
We recommend annual assessments to track progress and identify new opportunities. Institutions undergoing rapid AI implementation may benefit from semi-annual assessments during active implementation phases.
Are the generated policies legally binding?
Generated policies are templates and recommendations that require customization and legal review before implementation. They provide a strong foundation but must be adapted to your institution's specific context and legal requirements.
What support is available after completing the assessment?
Support varies by tier. All assessments include the comprehensive report and recommendations. Higher tiers include consultation calls, implementation support, and ongoing guidance. Additional consulting services are available separately.
How does this assessment differ from other AI readiness tools?
Our assessment is specifically designed for higher education and includes unique features like automated policy generation, academic integrity focus, student success alignment, and learning outcome preservation. It's the most comprehensive assessment available for colleges and universities.
