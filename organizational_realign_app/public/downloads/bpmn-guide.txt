BPMN Diagram Guide for Organizational Assessment
===============================================

What are BPMN Diagrams?
-----------------------
Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN) diagrams are standardized visual representations of business processes that help organizations understand, document, and improve their workflows.

Why We Need Your BPMN Diagrams:
-------------------------------
- Process complexity analysis
- Workflow efficiency assessment
- Redundancy identification
- Automation opportunity detection
- Resource allocation optimization

What to Include:
---------------
1. Core Business Processes:
   - Customer onboarding
   - Financial approvals
   - HR processes (hiring, performance reviews)
   - IT service delivery
   - Compliance workflows

2. Supporting Processes:
   - Communication flows
   - Decision-making processes
   - Escalation procedures
   - Quality assurance workflows

File Format Requirements:
------------------------
- Export as .bpmn, .xml, or image files (.png, .jpg, .svg)
- Include process descriptions/annotations
- Package all files in a single .zip archive

Tools We Support:
----------------
- Lucidchart
- Microsoft Visio
- Bizagi Modeler
- Camunda Modeler
- Draw.io (now diagrams.net)
- Any tool that exports standard BPMN 2.0

Don't Have BPMN Diagrams?
------------------------
No problem! Our assessment can still provide valuable insights. However, if you have informal process documentation, flowcharts, or procedure manuals, please include those instead.
